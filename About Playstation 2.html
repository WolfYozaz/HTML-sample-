Playstation 2

Facts

  • Released on March 4th, 2000
  • Discontinued in 2012

Procedures

  1. Pry open rubber cubes on the bottom of the console.
  2. Unscrew the screws under the rubber cubes.
  3. Take off top casing and cut off the sticker seal.
  4. Remove additional screws located on the left/right side
    from the optical drive in the four corners.
  5. Remove the power switch from back plate.
  6. Disconnect ribbon cable from the controller slot.
  7. Remove bottom casing.
